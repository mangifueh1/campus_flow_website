Privacy Policy

Your Academic Life, Simplified. Your Privacy, Respected.

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

At Campus Flow, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data to provide you with the best experience in managing your campus life.

1. Our Purpose

Campus Flow is built for students. We provide tools for timetable management, academic reminders, and campus navigation. Every piece of data we collect is used solely to enhance these academic productivity features.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

To provide personalized features and cloud sync, we collect:

  • Account Data: Name and email address provided during registration.
  • Academic Data: Timetables, course details, and reminders you create.

Device & App Usage

To ensure app stability and performance, we monitor:

  • Device type and operating system.
  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs.
  • Anonymized interactions with app features.

3. Permissions & Services

Location Access Used for campus maps, navigation between lectures, and location-based reminders.
Camera & Photos Used only when you choose to upload academic documents or capture notes.
Notifications Critical for class reminders, schedule changes, and important campus alerts.

4. Technology Partners

We use industry-standard infrastructure from Google Firebase to secure your data:

  • Authentication: Secure login management.
  • Firestore: Encrypted cloud storage for your academic data.
  • Cloud Messaging: Delivering timely push notifications.
  • Crashlytics: Monitoring app health to prevent data loss.

5. Data Security & Storage

Your data is stored using high-level encryption both in transit and at rest. We prioritize local storage for offline access, syncing with our secure servers only when an internet connection is available.
